Modern muscle cars

What makes a modern muscle car? These days, it takes a lot more than a big, honking engine under the hood. Today, enthusiasts can drive brawny cars that are both snarly and exhilarating, yet refined enough to serve as daily transportation. What is a muscle car? Purists will say a classic muscle car looks and drives like the all-American V8 sedans of the 1960s. They mean cars like the Dodge Charger, the Ford Mustang GT350, and the Chevy Camaro. Descendants of these original muscle cars do prowl the roads today. They’re still full of muscle — and have updated features for extra comfort.
